Schedule Intro

Integrative Wellness Coaching

Are you trying to discover your path, getting reacquainted with it or making a big shift? You don't have to stumble and explore it alone. Partner with an experienced guide to help uncover ideas and directions you may not have considered. A coach can offer alternative perspectives and help identify habits and behaviors that are keeping you from living your fullest life.

Happiness is much more than pleasure, power or achievement. It also doesn't come easy, let's work together and focus on five core things that historically have been shown to have an impact on happiness: emotional regulation, family, friendship, work and faith/connection. It's all about practice and making time for these things. Our modern world constantly pulls our attention away to more immediate pleasures and creates distractions from dealing with our pain. Make a shift, take the long road and invest in your future happiness.
